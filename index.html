Idaho

Idaho is a state located in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States, bordered by Montana to the northeast, Wyoming to the east, Nevada and Utah to the south, and Oregon and Washington to the west. It is the 14th largest state in the country by area and has a diverse geography, including mountain ranges, forests, lakes, and rivers. Its economy is largely driven by agriculture, including potato farming, and natural resource extraction industries such as lumber, mining, and energy production. Idaho is also known for outdoor recreational activities such as skiing, hiking, and fishing. The state capital is Boise, and its largest city is also Boise.

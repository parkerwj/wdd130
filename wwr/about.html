PURPOSE
To provide thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences that connect people with nature and foster a sense of adventure.
MISSION
To be the leading provider of rafting adventures in the region by offering a wide range of trips, exceptional service, and experienced guides who prioritize safety and fun. At Johnson Brothers Rafting, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of rafting. We are committed to providing safe, memorable and engaging trips that foster a sense of community and connection with nature. We believe in responsible stewardship of our wild rivers, and strive to leave the environment better than we found it.

History

Johnson Brothers Rafting was founded in 2015 by two brothers with a passion for adventure, Liam and Dean. Their love for the outdoors and white water rafting in particular inspired them to create a company that would bring the thrill of rafting to others. Starting with just a handful of rafts and a lot of determination, Liam and Dean set out to build a company that would provide safe and exciting rafting trips for people of all ages and skill levels. They focused on offering unique routes that took guests through some of the most breathtaking rivers and streams across the western United States. As word spread about Johnson Brothers Rafting's high-quality service, the company quickly began to grow. The brothers worked tirelessly to build a team of experienced guides who shared their love for rafting and had expertise on navigating different rivers and rapids. Today, Johnson Brothers Rafting is recognized as one of the top white water rafting companies in the country. With Liam and Dean still at the helm, the company is dedicated to providing unforgettable adventure experiences for its guests.The company's commitment to safety and customer satisfaction has earned it numerous awards and accolades. Johnson Brothers Rafting works closely with industry experts and governing bodies to maintain the highest levels of safety and to protect the environment through which they guide their trips.If you're looking for an adrenaline-pumping adventure on the water, come join us at Johnson Brothers Rafting. Our experienced guides and thrilling routes will ensure that you have an unforgettable trip, one you'll never forget.

AMWAJ RAFTING

PURPOSE
To provide thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences that connect people with nature and foster a sense of adventure.
MISSION
To be the leading provider of rafting adventures in the region by offering a wide range of trips, exceptional service, and experienced guides who prioritize safety and fun. At AMWAJ RAFTING, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of rafting. We are committed to providing safe, memorable and engaging trips that foster a sense of community and connection with nature. We believe in responsible stewardship of our wild rivers, and strive to leave the environment better than we found it.
MOTTO
"Experience the thrill, feel the rush, and make memories that last a lifetime."

History

"Founded in 2003, AMWAJ RAFTING has been providing thrilling whitewater rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. Our founder, Obaroakpo Billy Niesi, was passionate about outdoor recreation and saw an opportunity to share his love of rafting with others. With just a few rafts and a small team of guides, Billy began leading trips on some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the area.

Over the years, Rafting Adventures has grown to become a leading provider of rafting experiences in the region. We now offer a wide range of trips, from calm scenic floats to adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventures. Our team of experienced guides are some of the best in the business, with years of experience navigating the rapids and ensuring the safety of our guests.

Despite our growth, we remain committed to the same principles that Billy founded the company on. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of rafting, and we strive to provide exceptional service and unforgettable experiences to all of our guests. We look forward to continuing to share our passion for rafting with you for many years to come."

Overview

Purpose

At our rafting business, we believe that everyone should have the chance to experience the thrill of white-water rafting. That's why we've created an engaging online presence that makes it easy for you to discover and book the rafting experience that's right for you! By providing an excellent online experience, we aim to increase our visibility and reach a wider audience, allowing us to share our passion for rafting with adventurers everywhere. So whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, we invite you to join us for the ride of a lifetime!

Audience

Our water rafting website targets adventure-seekers and nature enthusiasts aged 18 to 35 who are looking for thrilling and adrenaline-pumping experiences. Scenario Question 1: What kind of activities would appeal to the target audience of this website? Answer 1: The target audience of this water rafting website is likely to be interested in activities such as white water rafting, kayaking, paddle-boarding, and other water-based sports. Scenario Question 2: What kinds of information would the target audience want to see on the website? Answer 2: The target audience of this website would likely want to see detailed descriptions of the different water rafting experiences available, along with information about pricing and availability. They may also want to see information about the location of the rafting sites, including any nearby accommodations and dining options. Scenario Question 3: What marketing messages would resonate with the target audience of this website? Answer 3: Marketing messages that emphasize the excitement and adventure of water rafting, while also highlighting the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape, are likely to resonate with the target audience of this website. Messaging that emphasizes safety and the expertise of the rafting guides may also be effective.
